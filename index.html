Projects

Health Hunch

Health Hunch is an innovative system designed to predict diseases by analyzing symptoms, enabling early detection and self-diagnosis. This award-winning project from the 2023 IBMZ Datathon harnesses advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning pipelines to accurately identify and classify diseases based on user-provided symptoms, paving the way for more accessible and proactive healthcare solutions.

Traffic Sign Classification

Developed a deep learning model to classify traffic signs using the LeNet architecture. This project aimed to enhance autonomous vehicle systems by accurately identifying and categorizing various traffic signs. The model leveraged convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to process and interpret traffic sign images, improving the vehicle's ability to navigate and respond to road signs effectively

Covid-19 Mortality Prediction Using Gradient Boosting

Developed a Gradient Boosting Regressor to predict Covid-19 death tolls in India, utilizing data from January 2020 to August 2021. Applied feature engineering, preprocessing, and hyperparameter tuning to optimize the model. Achieved accurate predictions, aiding in resource allocation and emergency preparedness.
